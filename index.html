VEHIC MOTOR COMPANY

Vehic Motor Company was started back in the 1980`s by Rolf Vehic.He was very passionate about cars but he was gretly disadvantaged by the fact that he was from a poor background.

Vehic went to France which wqas currently in those years quickly advancing in the field of inventions.Division the leading motor company rejeced him because he was nothing but a young boy with funny dreams.According to them the Wakandi and Pehenshi kin of car was not human possible.

Vehic had a dream and he was fired.There was no stopping him.Along with his friend Rodgers Amanda they set up a simple home garage where he begun working on the two models.
Vehic was so determined that despite his financial disadvantage he would sell rice balls day and night and save up to buy the materials he needed.
